The Problem

Designing a system that deal with data is not an easy task, it involves putting up with different data formats, modeling flexible schemas, data versioning, analisys , update , availability, and global distribution. Designing and Mantaining a system that does all that it’s at the same time expensive and extremely specialized. The result of all this is that only government and companies with deep pockets can afford being in the Data Business.

The solution

MyRing Data Suite it’s an enterprise solution for companies that need to assemble their Data solution. We take over all the complexity and allow low-tech personnel to implement complex Data solutions for their companies. We also help developers and small IT teams to ship faster and to never loose time again in redundant tasks so they can focus in improving their own systems.
